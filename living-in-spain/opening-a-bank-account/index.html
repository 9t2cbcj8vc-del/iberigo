Opening a Bank Account in Spain

A bank account can make rent, salary, utilities and everyday life easier. Requirements vary by bank, so this guide focuses on how to prepare.

Draft framework

English-first content framework. Detailed legal and route-specific content will be checked during editorial review.

Quick Answer

Banks usually want to identify you, understand your address and know your customer profile. Exact document rules vary by bank and account type.
