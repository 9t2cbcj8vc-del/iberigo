Driving in Spain

Driving rules depend on where your licence was issued and whether you are visiting or living in Spain. This guide helps you know what to check next.

Draft framework

English-first content framework. Detailed legal and route-specific content will be checked during editorial review.

Quick Answer

Do not assume your licence position stays the same after you become resident. Check recognition, exchange and renewal rules for your licence country.
