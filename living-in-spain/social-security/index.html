Social Security in Spain

Social Security is important for work and many public-service records. This guide separates the number from work registration so the first step is clearer.

Draft framework

English-first content framework. Detailed legal and route-specific content will be checked during editorial review.

Quick Answer

The Social Security number identifies your file. Being registered as active for employment or self-employment is a related but separate work step.

Real Questions People Ask

Can I obtain the number with a digital certificate?

Yes, digital access can be one route for some Social Security services.

Does my employer do this?

Often the employer handles employment registration, but you should confirm.

Is this the same as tax registration?

No. Tax and Social Security are separate systems.
