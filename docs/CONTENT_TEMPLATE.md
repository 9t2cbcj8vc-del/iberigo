# IberiGo Content Template

This is the standard structure for IberiGo core guides.

Use this for practical guides such as padrón, healthcare, banking, taxes, digital certificate, Social Security, driving and accommodation.

## Guide Structure

Every core guide should contain these sections, in this order:

1. Hero
2. Quick Answer
3. At a Glance
4. Before You Start
5. Who Needs This?
6. Official Requirements
7. Practical Advice
8. Step-by-Step Process
9. Common Mistakes
10. Real Questions People Ask
11. What Happens Next?
12. Continue Your Journey

## Section Purpose

### Hero

State the main question the page answers.

Keep the intro short and calm.

### Quick Answer

Give the user the plain-English answer first.

Do not bury the key point.

### At a Glance

Use a compact table for the most important facts.

### Before You Start

Use checklist-style cards.

Help the user understand what to prepare before going into details.

### Who Needs This?

Explain who the guide is for and who may need a different guide.

### Official Requirements

Separate official requirements from practical advice.

Do not guess. If detail needs checking, use:

> Detailed content coming during editorial review.

### Practical Advice

Explain what people usually need to think about in real life.

Keep it clear that this is practical guidance, not legal advice.

### Step-by-Step Process

Use numbered steps.

Keep each step short.

### Common Mistakes

Warn about avoidable problems.

Do not scare the reader.

### Real Questions People Ask

Use simple question-and-answer cards.

Answer only what can be answered safely.

### What Happens Next?

Explain the natural next step after the guide.

### Continue Your Journey

Link to related IberiGo guides.

Use descriptive buttons, never generic labels such as "Read more."

## Roadmap Structure

Every roadmap should contain:

1. Hero
2. Quick Facts
3. How to use this guide
4. Phase 1
5. Phase 2
6. Phase 3
7. You're Ready
8. Related Guides

## Draft Content Rule

If legal, tax, immigration or local-procedure detail is not confirmed, do not invent it.

Use:

> Detailed content coming during editorial review.
