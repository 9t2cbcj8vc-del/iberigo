# IberiGo Style Guide

## Voice

IberiGo should feel calm, practical and trustworthy.

Write like a helpful person who understands Spanish bureaucracy, not like a legal document.

## Writing Style

- Use plain English.
- Use short sentences.
- Explain jargon.
- Avoid unnecessary legal language.
- Prefer bullets over long paragraphs.
- Use tables and checklists where they make the page easier to scan.
- Put the answer before the explanation.

## Editorial Rules

- One page answers one primary question.
- Explain the process before the details.
- Separate official requirements from practical advice.
- Never overwhelm the reader.
- End with a clear next step.
- Do not guess legal, tax or immigration requirements.

## Terminology

Explain Spanish terms the first time they appear.

Examples:

- Padrón: town hall address registration.
- NIE: foreigner identification number.
- TIE: physical residence card generally used by non-EU nationals.
- Cl@ve: Spanish digital access system for public services.

## Buttons and Links

Use descriptive button labels.

Good:

- View the Padrón Guide
- View the Healthcare Guide
- View the Banking Guide
- Check the Official Source

Avoid:

- Read more
- Click here
- More info

## Warnings

Use warnings for genuine risk:

- Deadlines
- Unverified requirements
- Scams
- Confusing similar processes
- Situations where professional advice may be needed

Warnings should be useful, not dramatic.

## Tip Boxes

Use tip boxes for practical advice that saves time or avoids confusion.

Tips should be short and actionable.

## Mobile-First Layout

Pages should work well on phones first.

Use:

- Short sections
- Cards
- Tables that stack on mobile
- Clear buttons
- Large enough touch targets

Avoid:

- Dense paragraphs
- Wide tables that overflow
- Multiple competing calls to action

## Spanish Content

English is the source of truth first.

Spanish follows after English is complete and reviewed.

Spanish content should eventually answer the needs of Spanish-speaking Latin American users, not merely translate English pages word for word.
