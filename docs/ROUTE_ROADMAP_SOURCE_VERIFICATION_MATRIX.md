# Route Roadmap Source Verification Matrix

**Status:** Official-source verification pass completed and used as the basis for an explicit owner publication decision (`docs/ROUTE_ROADMAP_PUBLICATION_DECISION.md`); the technical launch has now been prepared on branch/PR `guide-system/route-roadmap-launch`, not yet merged. **This verification is not a substitute for professional legal advice** — it is an owner-controlled verification process, performed because a qualified professional legal/immigration review was not available.
**Date prepared:** 2026-07-11
**Method:** Each page's main claims were checked against official Spanish government sources (BOE, Ministerio de Inclusión/Migraciones, consular pages, Seguridad Social), using the source hierarchy below. Secondary sources (law firms, blogs) were consulted only for orientation, never as the basis for any claim on the pages.

## Source hierarchy used

1. BOE legislation and regulations — Ley Orgánica 4/2000 (as amended) and Real Decreto 1155/2024 (Reglamento de Extranjería, in force since 2026-05-20, replacing RD 557/2011).
2. Spanish Government / Ministry pages — `mites.gob.es`, `interior.gob.es`.
3. Extranjería / Ministerio de Inclusión, Seguridad Social y Migraciones pages — `inclusion.gob.es`, `extranjeros.inclusion.gob.es`, `inclusion.seg-social.es` (the "Hoja" procedure sheets for each authorization type).
4. Ministerio de Asuntos Exteriores consular visa pages — `exteriores.gob.es/Consulados/...`.
5. Seguridad Social official pages — `seg-social.es`, `prestaciones.seg-social.es` (used for the S1 form).
6. EU official sources for EU citizen/free-movement context, and `sede.policia.gob.es` for the EU registration certificate procedure.
7. Secondary sources (law-firm articles, immigration blogs) — used only to locate which official page covers a topic, or to flag a recent regulatory change worth confirming against an official source; never cited as authority on the pages themselves.

Access note: direct `WebFetch` of `extranjeros.inclusion.gob.es` failed in this environment (TLS certificate verification error on that specific host). Verification was instead performed via `WebSearch`, which surfaced and quoted the relevant official pages (`inclusion.gob.es`, `seg-social.es`, `sede.policia.gob.es`, `exteriores.gob.es`) directly, including their published requirements text. Where a claim could not be independently confirmed against an official source, it is marked "unclear / keep cautious" below rather than assumed correct.

It does **not** replace a qualified professional legal/immigration review, and it does **not** constitute legal advice to IberiGo's readers or to IberiGo itself.
