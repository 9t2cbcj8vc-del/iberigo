# IberiGo Project Principles

## Mission

Help people move to Spain with confidence through clear, practical and trustworthy guidance.

## Audience

### Primary

People moving to Spain.

### Secondary

People already living in Spain.

Content is written for ordinary people, not legal professionals.

## Core Principles

1. Accuracy over speed.
2. Simplicity over complexity.
3. One page answers one primary question.
4. Separate official requirements from practical advice.
5. Every guide ends with a clear next step.
6. Explain the process before the details.
7. Never overwhelm the reader.
8. Mobile-first.
9. English first. Spanish follows after English is complete.
10. Quality is more important than quantity.

## Product Philosophy

We do not try to answer every question on one page.

We answer one question exceptionally well, then guide the visitor to the next step.

## Development Workflow

1. Research
2. Write
3. Review
4. Implement
5. Preview
6. Correct
7. Publish

## Publishing Rule

Nothing goes to production without:

- Content review
- UX review
- Mobile review
- Accuracy review

## Long-Term Vision

Become the most trusted free English-language resource for people moving to Spain.
