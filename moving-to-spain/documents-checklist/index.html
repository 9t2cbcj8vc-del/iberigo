Documents Checklist for Moving to Spain

Documents are where many moves slow down. This checklist gives you a calm starting point before you go into route-specific details.

Draft framework

English-first content framework. Detailed legal and route-specific content will be checked during editorial review.

Quick Answer

Start with identity, address, money, healthcare and purpose evidence. Route-specific document rules should be checked separately before filing anything.

Before You Start

1

Know your main route or likely situation.

2

Check document expiry dates.

3

Keep digital scans and paper copies.

Tip

Use file names that make sense: passport, rental-contract, healthcare-proof, employment-contract, funds-proof.

Who Needs This?

1

People preparing a move to Spain.

2

People comparing possible visa, registration or residence routes.

3

People who want a simple document folder before appointments.

Important

Practical Advice

1

Do not wait until the appointment week to request foreign certificates.

2

Use consistent names and spellings across documents.

3

Track where each document came from and when it expires.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. 1

    List your likely route.

  2. 2

    Collect identity and civil documents.

  3. 3

    Collect address, money, work, study or family evidence.

  4. 4

    Check whether translations or apostilles may be needed.

  5. 5

    Store everything in one folder before booking appointments.

Common Mistakes

1

Using a generic checklist as a final legal document list.

2

Forgetting expiry dates.

3

Submitting screenshots where official documents are required.
